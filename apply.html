GiveCard Launch Application

Welcome to GiveCard! You are applying to join GiveCard’s pilot program. If selected, you will receive $250 a month for 3 months as well as access to our other financial resources. This application should take 5-10 minutes to complete, but feel free to take all the time you need. GiveCard is a startup non-profit organization seeking to help individuals facing homelessness achieve financial stability by distributing debit cards. To learn more about GiveCard, please visit our website.

Participation in this program is part of GiveCard’s primary launch. As a participant, you will be required to correspond with the GiveCard team regarding your finances during the trial period. All data will be kept completely confidential.

We look forward to hearing from you and will be in touch about next steps soon!

Mission

We’re a nonprofit organization giving specialized debit cards, digital donation systems and revolutionary financial planning to the homeless.
